Regex engine

There are basically only two kinds of regular expression engines: text-directed engines, and regex-directed engines (NFA). Nearly all modern regex (DFA) flavors are based on regex-directed engines (NFA).

Regex engine is eager, this means it will stop searching as soon as it finds a valid match.
